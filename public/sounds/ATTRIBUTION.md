# Sound effect sources

The “Guess the sound” minigame uses real recordings from Wikimedia Commons. The source, creator, and license are also displayed inside the game after each answer is revealed.

- `dog.ogg` — “Barking of a dog” by Amada44, licensed under [CC BY-SA 3.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/3.0/). [Source](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Barking_of_a_dog.ogg).
- “WWS CarPrinzNSU1200Cengine” by Work With Sounds / Technical Museum of Slovenia, licensed under [CC BY 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/). [Source](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:WWS_CarPrinzNSU1200Cengine.ogg).
- “4000 class train horn” by Cityedit14, licensed under [CC BY-SA 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/). [Source](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:4000_class_train_horn.ogg).

The remaining recordings are marked as Public Domain or CC0 on their linked Wikimedia Commons file pages.
